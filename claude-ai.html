What is Claude AI?

Claude is Anthropic's AI assistant — widely regarded as the best AI for nuanced, long-form writing, complex analysis and tasks requiring careful reasoning. Unlike some AI tools that produce generic output, Claude is known for producing thoughtful, well-structured responses that feel genuinely written rather than generated.

Claude excels at editing and improving existing text, summarising long documents, writing in a specific tone or style, coding assistance, research synthesis and complex question answering. Its 200,000-token context window means it can read and work with extremely long documents in a single session.

Claude Pro ($20/month) gives priority access, longer conversations and access to Claude's most powerful models. For professionals who write, research or analyse content daily, Claude is one of the highest-value AI subscriptions available.
