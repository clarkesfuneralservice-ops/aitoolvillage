Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI write marketing emails?
Yes — ChatGPT, Jasper and Copy.ai all write marketing emails in seconds. Give them your product, audience and goal and they generate full email drafts including subject lines, body copy and CTAs. Most email marketers use AI to draft then edit for brand voice.
What is the best AI tool for email subject lines?
ChatGPT is the best free tool — prompt it with 'write 10 email subject lines for [your topic] targeting [your audience]'. Copy.ai also has a dedicated subject line generator template. Always A/B test AI-generated subject lines.
How does AI improve email open rates?
AI improves open rates primarily through better subject lines (tested at scale), optimal send time prediction and personalisation at scale. AI writing tools also produce cleaner, more engaging copy that reduces unsubscribes.
