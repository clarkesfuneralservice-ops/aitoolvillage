Frequently Asked Questions
Jasper AI is the best for professional marketing teams who need brand-consistent content at scale. For individuals and freelancers, ChatGPT or Claude offer the best value — both have capable free plans. Grammarly is the best for improving existing writing rather than generating new content.
ChatGPT's free plan is the most capable free writing tool — it handles any format with high quality output. Grammarly's free plan is best for grammar checking and editing. QuillBot's free plan is best for paraphrasing and summarising. For SEO writing, most tools require a paid plan.
AI-written content can rank well when it's high quality, well-structured and genuinely useful to readers. Google's guidance is that it rewards helpful content regardless of how it was produced. The key is editing AI output to add real expertise, specific data and original insights — pure unedited AI content tends to be too generic to rank well competitively.
For high-volume, formulaic content — product descriptions, social captions, ad variations — AI tools have largely replaced the need for copywriters. For strategic brand writing, nuanced storytelling and content requiring genuine expertise and original research, experienced writers remain essential. Most copywriters now use AI tools to work faster rather than competing against them.
