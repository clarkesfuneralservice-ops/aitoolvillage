Frequently Asked Questions
TaxJar is the best sales tax automation tool for most small businesses and ecommerce sellers — it calculates the correct rate for every transaction, files returns automatically across all US states and alerts you when you cross economic nexus thresholds in new states. For enterprise businesses with global operations, Avalara is the more powerful choice.
FreshBooks and QuickBooks both offer 30-day free trials and handle the bookkeeping that feeds into tax preparation. For freelancers specifically, Keeper Tax finds missed deductions automatically. There's no fully free comprehensive tax tool, but FreshBooks' trial gives you enough time to assess whether it fits your workflow.
AI tax tools help in several ways: automatically categorising transactions into the right expense categories, identifying deductible expenses you might miss, calculating correct sales tax rates in real time, filing returns automatically, monitoring nexus thresholds across states and generating the reports your accountant needs. The biggest time savings come from year-round automation rather than scrambling at tax time.
For most small businesses, AI bookkeeping tools like FreshBooks or QuickBooks handle day-to-day accounting well. However, a CPA or tax professional is still valuable for strategic tax planning, handling complex situations (business structure changes, major asset purchases, audits) and ensuring compliance with changing tax law. AI tools reduce the hours your accountant needs to spend on data entry, lowering your accounting bill.
