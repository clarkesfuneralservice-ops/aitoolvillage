🏆 Our Verdict
Depends on your needs

Xero wins for growing businesses that need accountant collaboration and deep integrations. FreshBooks wins for freelancers and service businesses that prioritise invoicing and ease of use.

Pros & Cons

📒 Xero — Pros

  • Unlimited users on all plans
  • 600+ third-party integrations
  • Strong inventory management
  • Excellent accountant tools
  • Multi-currency support

📒 Xero — Cons

  • Steeper learning curve
  • No phone support
  • US payroll costs extra

🧾 FreshBooks — Pros

  • Best-in-class invoicing UI
  • Built-in time tracking
  • Excellent client portal
  • Award-winning customer support
  • Easy to use from day one

🧾 FreshBooks — Cons

  • Limited users on base plans
  • Weaker inventory features
  • Fewer integrations than Xero
