🏆 Our Verdict
Midjourney

Midjourney produces the highest quality images and is the industry standard for creative professionals. Leonardo AI wins if you need a free tier, custom model training, or a web-based interface without Discord.

Pros & Cons

🎨 Midjourney — Pros

  • Highest quality, most photorealistic output
  • Huge active community on Discord
  • V6 model is industry-leading
  • Great for brand & marketing imagery
  • Constant model improvements

🎨 Midjourney — Cons

  • No free tier currently
  • Discord-only interface (no web app)
  • Less control over specific elements
  • Can't train on your own images cheaply

🖼️ Leonardo AI — Pros

  • Generous free tier (150 tokens/day)
  • Train custom AI models on your images
  • Web-based interface (no Discord needed)
  • Fine-grained style control
  • Great for consistent character/product images

🖼️ Leonardo AI — Cons

  • Quality slightly below Midjourney V6
  • Free plan has watermarks & limits
  • Can be slower on free tier
  • More complex UI for beginners
