Frequently Asked Questions

How is AI changing bookkeeping?
AI is automating the most time-consuming bookkeeping tasks: bank transaction categorisation, receipt data extraction, bank reconciliation and report generation. Bookkeepers who adopt AI tools are handling 2–3x more clients without additional staff.
What is the best AI bookkeeping software?
Xero is the top choice for professional bookkeepers managing multiple clients — its AI bank reconciliation and partner portal are industry-leading. For solo bookkeepers with smaller clients, Wave Accounting (free) or Zoho Books (free under $50k) are excellent starting points.
Can AI replace bookkeepers?
No — AI handles repetitive data entry and transaction matching, but bookkeepers are still needed for client relationships, complex categorisation decisions, tax strategy and financial analysis. AI makes bookkeepers more efficient, not redundant.
