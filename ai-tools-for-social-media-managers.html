Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free AI tool for social media?
Buffer is the best free AI social media scheduling tool — it schedules posts across all major platforms at no cost. Pair it with Canva AI for free image creation and ChatGPT for caption writing for a complete free social media AI stack.
Can AI write social media captions?
Yes — ChatGPT and Copy.ai both write social media captions in seconds. Give them your topic, brand tone and platform and they generate multiple options. Most social media managers use AI to draft captions then edit for brand voice.
What AI tools do social media managers use most?
The most widely used are Buffer or Hootsuite for scheduling, Canva AI for graphics, ChatGPT for copywriting and Midjourney or Leonardo AI for image generation. Most professionals use 3–4 tools in combination.
