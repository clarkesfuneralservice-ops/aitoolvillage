What is Frase IO?

Frase is an AI-powered SEO content tool built specifically for content writers and SEO teams — it combines research, brief creation, content writing and optimisation into one workflow. The core idea is simple: instead of spending hours researching a topic before writing, Frase analyses the top-ranking pages for your target keyword and generates a comprehensive content brief in minutes.

Frase's SERP analysis examines the top 20 results for any keyword — extracting the headers, questions, topics and word counts that Google is rewarding. It then generates a content brief that tells you exactly what to cover, what questions to answer and how long your article needs to be to compete. This brief becomes your writing guide.

The AI writer generates content based on the brief, and the real-time content optimisation score shows how well your article covers the topic compared to top-ranking competitors — turning green as you add the right topics and sections. For content teams producing SEO articles at scale, Frase dramatically speeds up the research and brief-creation stages of the workflow.

Frequently Asked Questions
Frase is best for content writers and SEO teams who produce blog content at scale. Its strongest feature is SERP research and content brief generation — analysing top-ranking pages for a keyword and creating a comprehensive brief in minutes. If your bottleneck is research and briefing rather than writing speed, Frase delivers the highest ROI of any content tool.
For teams producing 4+ SEO articles per month, yes — Frase saves 2-3 hours per article on research and briefing. At $115/month for 30 documents, that's under $4 per brief. If you're billing clients for content strategy or producing content at scale, the time savings easily justify the cost. For solo bloggers producing 1-2 articles per month, the Solo plan at $45/month may be harder to justify.
Frase and Surfer SEO are the two leading AI SEO content tools and are often compared directly. Frase is stronger at research and brief creation — its SERP analysis and question research are more comprehensive. Surfer SEO is stronger at real-time content scoring and has better keyword research integration. Many serious content teams use both together: Frase for research and briefs, Surfer for optimisation scoring while writing.
Frase doesn't have a permanent free plan, but offers a 5-day full-access trial for $1. This is enough time to run several SERP analyses, create content briefs and test the AI writer. After the trial, plans start at $45/month for the Solo plan with 4 documents per month.
