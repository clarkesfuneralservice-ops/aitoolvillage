How to Choose

For short-form content (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)

CapCut is free with no watermark and includes AI auto-captions and viral templates — the obvious starting point. OpusClip is ideal if you're repurposing long videos into clips.

For podcasts and talking-head video

Descript's transcript-based editing is the most efficient workflow available — edit by editing text, and Overdub fixes verbal mistakes without re-recording.

For repurposing long content at volume

Recapo.ai combines clipping, scripts, subtitles and voiceover in one workflow with batch export — useful for creators maintaining a high posting frequency without a team.

For professional production

Adobe Premiere Pro remains the industry standard, now with AI features like auto reframe and enhanced speech that speed up professional workflows without sacrificing control.
