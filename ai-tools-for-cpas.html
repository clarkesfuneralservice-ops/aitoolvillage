Frequently Asked Questions

What AI tools are CPAs using in 2026?
The most widely adopted AI tools among CPAs are Xero or QuickBooks for cloud accounting, Dext for document capture, Gusto for payroll management, Fathom HQ for client reporting and ChatGPT for drafting client communications. Most CPA practices are now using 4–6 AI tools regularly.
Can AI prepare tax returns?
AI significantly speeds up tax preparation by automating data extraction, categorisation and report generation. However, CPAs are still required for final review, complex tax strategy and client sign-off. AI handles the data work; CPAs handle the judgement.
What is the best practice management software for CPAs?
ClickUp is the best free practice management option for small CPA firms. For larger practices, dedicated tools like Karbon or TaxDome provide more CPA-specific workflow management. Most CPAs combine a dedicated practice tool with Xero for client accounting.
