AI Writing Tools for Marketing

Content creation is where AI delivers the most immediate ROI for marketing teams. The tools below dramatically reduce the time it takes to produce blogs, emails, ad copy and social content — without sacrificing quality.

1. Jasper AI
Best overall AI marketing writer
🏆 Best Overall

Jasper is the most complete AI writing platform for marketing teams. Its Brand Voice feature learns your company's tone and applies it consistently across everything it writes — blogs, emails, social posts and ad copy. With 50+ marketing-specific templates and deep Surfer SEO integration, Jasper is purpose-built for marketing in a way that general AI tools aren't.

Where Jasper excels is long-form content at scale. Marketing teams at HubSpot, Canva and Shopify use Jasper to produce 10x more content without proportionally growing their writing teams. The quality is consistently high enough to publish with light editing rather than heavy rewrites.

Best for: Marketing teams producing high volumes of blog posts, emails and campaign copy. Not ideal for: Solo users who only write occasionally — the cost is harder to justify.

From $49/mo · 7-day free trial Read Full Review →
2. ChatGPT
Best free AI marketing assistant
FREE

ChatGPT remains the most versatile marketing AI available — and the free version (GPT-4o) is genuinely powerful enough for most marketing tasks. From writing email subject lines and ad copy to brainstorming campaign ideas and drafting entire blog posts, ChatGPT handles the full range of marketing writing tasks through natural conversation.

The key to getting great marketing output from ChatGPT is prompt quality. Give it your brand voice guidelines, target audience details and specific format requirements and the output is remarkably good. Use it for first drafts, ideation, repurposing content and tasks that don't require a specialised tool.

Best for: Any marketer — free, immediate and handles almost any writing task. Not ideal for: Highly structured templates or brand voice consistency without careful prompting.

3. Writesonic
Best value AI content writer
Best Value

Writesonic gives you 10,000 free words per month — the most generous free plan of any AI writing tool. Its SEO mode analyses search intent and competitor content as you write, making it particularly strong for blog content that needs to rank. Chatsonic adds real-time web search so content references current information rather than training data.

AI SEO Tools for Marketing

SEO remains the highest-ROI marketing channel for most businesses — organic traffic compounds over time unlike paid ads. These AI tools make it dramatically easier to create content that ranks.

4. Surfer SEO
Best AI SEO content tool
🏆 Top Pick

Surfer SEO is the most effective tool for writing content that ranks on Google. It analyses the top 20 results for your target keyword and gives you a real-time content score based on word count, keyword density, headings structure and semantic terms. Write in Surfer's editor and it tells you exactly what to add to outrank competitors.

The Surfer + Jasper integration is particularly powerful — Jasper writes the first draft and Surfer optimises it for SEO simultaneously. Marketing teams using both consistently see page 1 rankings for new content within weeks rather than months.

From $89/mo · Free trial Read Full Review →
5. Semrush
Best all-in-one SEO platform
🏆 Top Pick

Semrush is the industry standard for SEO research — keyword research, competitor analysis, backlink auditing and site technical audit all in one platform. Its 25 billion keyword database is the largest available, and its competitor intelligence shows you exactly which keywords your competitors rank for so you can target the same opportunities.

From $139/mo · 7-day free trial Read Full Review →

AI Social Media Tools

Social media is one of the most time-intensive marketing channels — constant content demands, platform-specific formatting and optimal posting times. These AI tools dramatically reduce the time required.

6. Buffer
Best AI social media scheduler
FREE PLAN

Buffer's AI assistant generates platform-specific captions from a brief description and schedules them at optimal times based on your audience's activity. The free plan covers 3 social channels — Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter and others — making it the most accessible social media AI tool available. For small businesses and solo creators, Buffer's free plan covers most needs.

Free — 3 channels · From $6/mo per channel Read Full Review →
7. Predis.ai
Best AI social content generator
FREE PLAN

Predis.ai generates complete social media posts — both the visual design and the caption — from a single text prompt. Unlike tools that only write captions or only generate images, Predis produces ready-to-post packages for Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook and TikTok. Particularly useful for product-focused businesses that need branded visual content at volume.

Free 15 posts/mo · From $29/mo Read Full Review →

AI Email Marketing Tools

Email marketing delivers the highest ROI of any digital channel — $36 for every $1 spent on average. AI makes email significantly more effective by personalising content, optimising send times and automating complex sequences.

8. ActiveCampaign
Best AI email automation platform
🏆 Top Pick

ActiveCampaign combines email marketing, CRM and AI automation into one platform. Its AI features include predictive sending (learns the optimal send time for each individual subscriber), predictive content (shows different email content to different segments automatically) and AI-generated email copy. Used by 180,000+ businesses, it's the most capable mid-market email platform available.

From $15/mo · 14-day free trial Read Full Review →
9. Mailchimp
Best free email marketing tool
FREE PLAN

Mailchimp's free plan covers up to 500 contacts and includes AI subject line optimisation, send time prediction and pre-built automation templates. For small businesses starting with email marketing, Mailchimp's free plan provides everything needed to get started. The drag-and-drop email builder requires no design skills and produces professional results.

Free — 500 contacts · From $13/mo Read Full Review →
