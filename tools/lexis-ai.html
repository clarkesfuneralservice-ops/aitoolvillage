What is Lexis+ AI?

Lexis+ AI is the generative AI layer on top of LexisNexis — the world's largest legal database with 150+ years of legal content. It lets lawyers ask natural language questions and get cited answers drawn from case law, statutes, regulations and secondary sources in seconds.

Unlike general AI tools that can hallucinate legal citations, Lexis+ AI only cites sources that actually exist in the LexisNexis database — every answer is grounded in verified legal content. It also drafts documents, summarises cases and compares legal standards across jurisdictions.

For lawyers already subscribed to LexisNexis, Lexis+ AI is the natural way to add AI to their research workflow without switching platforms or risking hallucinated citations.
