What is Lofty?

Lofty (formerly Chime) is an all-in-one AI platform built specifically for real estate agents and teams — combining CRM, IDX website, lead generation, AI follow-up and transaction management in one subscription. It's designed to solve the biggest challenge in real estate: responding to leads fast enough to convert them.

Its AI features include an AI assistant that texts and emails new leads within seconds of inquiry, nurtures long-term prospects with personalised follow-up sequences, and flags hot leads to agents when buying signals appear. The AI analyses lead behaviour on the IDX website — which properties they're viewing, how often they return — and uses this to personalise outreach.

For solo agents and small teams who can't afford a dedicated ISA (inside sales agent) to follow up on every lead, Lofty's AI essentially acts as that role — ensuring no lead goes cold from slow response time, which is the #1 reason real estate leads don't convert.

Frequently Asked Questions
Lofty is used by real estate agents and teams as their central platform for lead management. Its primary value is AI-powered lead follow-up — when a prospect fills out a form on your IDX website or a lead gen platform, Lofty's AI texts them within seconds and engages in a conversation to qualify them and book an appointment. It also handles long-term nurture, pipeline tracking and transaction management.
At $449/month, Lofty is a significant expense for a solo agent. The ROI calculation depends on your lead volume and conversion rate. If you're generating 20+ leads per month and closing 1-2 more per year due to AI follow-up, the maths usually work. For part-time agents or those generating fewer than 10 leads/month, the price is harder to justify.
Lofty is more of an all-in-one platform — CRM plus IDX website plus AI follow-up in one subscription. Follow Up Boss is a more focused CRM that integrates with separate lead sources and websites. Lofty makes more sense if you want one vendor; Follow Up Boss makes more sense if you already have a website and lead sources and just need a powerful CRM to manage them.
