What is Predis.ai?

Predis.ai is an AI social media content generator that creates complete posts — both the visual design and the caption — from a single text description. Unlike tools that only write captions or only generate images, Predis.ai produces ready-to-post content packages for Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter and TikTok in one workflow.

You describe what you want to post — for example, 'a promotional post for our summer sale, 30% off all products, target audience: young professionals' — and Predis.ai generates multiple post variations with branded visuals, relevant captions and suggested hashtags. You pick the best variation, make any edits and schedule or export directly.

Predis.ai also includes a competitor analysis feature that shows what content is performing best for similar businesses in your niche, and a content calendar that suggests what types of posts to create on which days based on your audience's engagement patterns. This strategic layer makes it more than just a content generator — it's a social media planning tool.

Frequently Asked Questions
Canva gives more design control and produces higher quality visuals — it's the better choice when design quality matters most. Predis.ai is faster for generating complete post packages at volume — if you need 20 posts per week across multiple platforms, Predis.ai's AI generation is significantly faster than designing each one in Canva. Many teams use both: Predis.ai for volume, Canva for hero content.
Predis.ai has a free plan that includes 15 AI-generated posts per month — enough to evaluate whether the output quality works for your brand. Paid plans start at $29/month for 100 posts. Annual billing reduces the cost. The free plan is genuinely useful for small businesses posting 3-4 times per week.
Predis.ai creates content formatted for Instagram (feed posts, stories, reels), LinkedIn (posts and carousels), Facebook, Twitter/X and TikTok. Each format is sized correctly for the platform automatically. You can create content for multiple platforms from the same prompt and Predis.ai adapts the format and copy for each one.
