What is Expensify?

Expensify is the leading AI expense management platform used by 15 million+ people worldwide. Its SmartScan technology uses AI to read receipt photos taken on your phone — automatically extracting the merchant, date, amount and category — and creating an expense report entry in seconds. Manual data entry is essentially eliminated.

For finance and accounting teams, Expensify automates the approval workflow — expenses route to the right manager, policy violations are flagged automatically and approved expenses sync directly to QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite and other accounting platforms. Month-end expense reconciliation that used to take days now takes hours.

Expensify Card takes this further — it's a corporate card where every transaction is automatically matched to a receipt, categorised and reported in real time. Finance teams see company spending as it happens, not 30 days later.
