What is Elicit?

Elicit is an AI research assistant built for academics. It automates the most time-consuming parts of literature reviews — finding relevant papers, extracting specific data points and summarising findings across multiple studies. Researchers use it to cut literature review time from weeks to hours.

Key Features

Academic paper search and filtering
Data extraction from papers
Cross-paper synthesis and summaries
Custom column extraction
CSV export of extracted data
Citation management
PDF upload and analysis
Research question refinement
