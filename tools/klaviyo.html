What is Klaviyo?

Klaviyo is the most popular email and SMS marketing platform for ecommerce — used by 150,000+ brands including major Shopify stores. It's built specifically for ecommerce, meaning every feature is designed around the way online stores acquire, retain and grow customers.

Its AI features include predictive analytics that identify which customers are likely to churn, AI-generated email subject lines and copy, smart send-time optimisation and product recommendation blocks that auto-populate with items each individual customer is most likely to buy.

The real power is in automated flows — abandoned cart sequences, post-purchase follow-ups, win-back campaigns and browse abandonment emails that run automatically 24/7. Most Klaviyo users report email and SMS contributing 30-50% of their total revenue.
