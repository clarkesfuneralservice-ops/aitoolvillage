What is Jenni AI?

Jenni AI is an AI writing assistant built specifically for students, academics and researchers — it helps you write essays, research papers, literature reviews and dissertations faster without plagiarism concerns. Unlike general AI writing tools, Jenni is designed around the needs of academic writing: it generates in-text citations, supports multiple citation styles (APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard) and keeps your writing original.

The core feature is AI autocomplete — Jenni suggests the next sentence or paragraph as you write, so you're always in control of the content while the AI handles the heavy lifting of generating text. You can accept, reject or edit every suggestion, keeping your voice and ideas central to the work.

Jenni also lets you upload your own research papers and PDFs — it reads them and can generate text that cites your sources directly, grounding AI output in real academic literature. For students who need to write well-cited, original academic content quickly, Jenni AI is one of the most purpose-built tools available.
