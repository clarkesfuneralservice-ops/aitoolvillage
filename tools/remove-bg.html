What is Remove.bg?

Remove.bg is a specialised AI tool that detects and removes the background from any image automatically. Upload a photo, wait five seconds, download the result. It handles complex edges like hair, fur and fine details that traditionally required hours of manual Photoshop work.

For marketers, it's essential for creating clean product images, transparent-background logos and polished social media visuals. The free plan downloads at lower resolution (which is fine for web/social). The paid plan gives full HD downloads. A browser extension and Figma plugin are also available.
