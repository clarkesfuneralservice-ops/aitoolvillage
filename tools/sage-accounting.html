What is Sage Accounting?

Sage Accounting (formerly Sage Business Cloud Accounting) is cloud-based accounting software aimed at small businesses and sole traders, offering invoicing, expense tracking, bank reconciliation and VAT/tax reporting. As part of the long-established Sage product family, it's widely used in the UK and internationally, particularly by businesses with accountants already familiar with Sage products.

AI features include automatic bank feed categorisation that learns from past transactions, and Sage's broader AI assistant tools help answer accounting questions and flag anomalies. For businesses whose accountants prefer Sage, or those transitioning from Sage desktop products, Sage Accounting provides a familiar cloud upgrade path.
