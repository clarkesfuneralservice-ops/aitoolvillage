What is ActiveCampaign?

ActiveCampaign is an AI-powered email marketing and CRM platform used by 180,000+ businesses. It combines email automation, contact management, sales CRM and AI-driven personalisation into one platform — making it the go-to choice for businesses that have outgrown Mailchimp but don't need enterprise-level complexity.

ActiveCampaign's AI features include predictive sending (it learns the best time to send to each contact), predictive content (shows different content to different segments automatically), AI-generated email copy and automated lead scoring that identifies your most sales-ready contacts.

The visual automation builder is one of the most powerful available — you can create complex, multi-step workflows triggered by almost any behaviour, from email clicks to website visits to purchase history. For ecommerce businesses, ActiveCampaign's deep Shopify, WooCommerce and BigCommerce integrations make it especially valuable.
