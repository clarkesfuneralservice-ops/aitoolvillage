What is Spellbook?

Spellbook is an AI contract drafting tool that lives inside Microsoft Word as an add-in — it uses GPT-4 to suggest clauses, review contract language and draft entire sections without ever leaving your document. For lawyers who spend their days in Word, it's the most frictionless way to add AI to contract work.

It suggests missing clauses, flags aggressive or unusual terms, explains complex provisions in plain English and drafts alternative language on demand. Spellbook is trained on millions of legal contracts, so its suggestions are grounded in market-standard language rather than generic AI output.

Unlike enterprise CLM platforms, Spellbook is designed for individual lawyers and small teams — it's quick to set up, affordable relative to enterprise tools and immediately useful for anyone doing regular contract work.
