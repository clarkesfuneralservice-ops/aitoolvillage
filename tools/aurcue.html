What is Aurcue?

Aurcue is an AI-powered personal style platform that transforms a single photo into actionable style guidance — colour analysis, outfit recommendations, hairstyle suggestions, glasses recommendations and seasonal styling advice. It bridges the gap between expensive personal stylists and generic fashion advice by delivering personalised recommendations based on your actual appearance.

The platform's colour analysis feature identifies your personal colour season — whether you're a Spring, Summer, Autumn or Winter — and uses this to recommend which clothing colours, hair tones and makeup shades will complement your natural colouring. This seasonal colour analysis has traditionally required an in-person consultation with a trained colour analyst; Aurcue makes it accessible from a single photo.

Aurcue is particularly valuable before making significant appearance changes — deciding whether to go blonde, choosing between glasses frames, investing in a new wardrobe season or completely updating your style. Instead of relying on gut feeling or generic online advice, you get analysis grounded in your specific colouring and features. The freemium model makes it accessible to try before committing to a paid plan.

Who is Aurcue for?

Aurcue is designed for anyone wanting to make more confident, informed decisions about their personal style — particularly people considering a hair colour change, buying a significant wardrobe update, choosing new glasses frames or wanting to understand which colours genuinely suit them. It's also useful for fashion enthusiasts and stylists wanting AI-assisted colour analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions
Colour season analysis is a personal styling framework that categorises people into four seasonal types — Spring, Summer, Autumn and Winter — based on their natural colouring (skin tone, hair colour, eye colour). Each season corresponds to a palette of colours that harmonise with those natural characteristics. Wearing colours from your season makes your complexion look clearer and more vibrant; wearing colours outside your season can make you look washed out. Aurcue uses AI to perform this analysis from your photo.
Aurcue offers a freemium model — basic style analysis features are available for free so you can evaluate the quality of recommendations before upgrading. Visit aurcue.com for current plan details and pricing as features available on each tier may change.
Accuracy depends primarily on photo quality — good natural lighting, a clear face photo without heavy filters and a neutral background produce the best results. The colour analysis is generally reliable for identifying warm vs cool undertones. Specific style recommendations should be treated as a personalised starting point for exploration rather than definitive prescriptions — personal style always involves subjective preferences that AI cannot fully account for.
