What is Intercom?

Intercom is an AI-first customer service platform — its flagship Fin AI agent uses GPT-4 to resolve customer questions instantly, drawing on your help centre, product documentation and support history. Fin resolves over 50% of support questions without human involvement for most customers, dramatically reducing support costs.

Beyond AI, Intercom provides live chat, a help centre builder, outbound messaging for product tours and announcements, and a full customer data platform that gives support agents full context about who they're talking to — their plan, usage, previous conversations and account history.

Intercom is the default choice for SaaS and tech companies — its in-app messenger is the most widely recognised in the industry and its AI capabilities are among the most mature of any customer service platform. Used by Notion, Atlassian, Amazon and thousands of software companies.

Frequently Asked Questions
Fin is Intercom's AI agent — built on GPT-4 and trained on your help centre articles, product documentation and support history. When a customer asks a question, Fin searches your knowledge base and provides an accurate, conversational answer instantly. If Fin can't resolve the issue confidently, it hands off to a human agent. Most Intercom customers see Fin resolve 40-60% of their support volume automatically.
Intercom works for ecommerce but isn't optimised for it the way Gorgias is. Gorgias has deeper Shopify integration, shows order data in tickets and has ecommerce-specific automations. Intercom is better suited to SaaS, tech products and subscription businesses where in-app messaging and customer lifecycle context matter more than order management.
Intercom starts at $39/seat/month for the Essential plan. The Advanced plan with more AI features and automation is $99/seat/month. Fin AI usage is charged separately per resolution on some plans. Pricing has historically been complex — check Intercom's current pricing page for the latest structure as it changes periodically.
