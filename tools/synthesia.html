What is Synthesia?

Synthesia is an AI video generation platform that creates professional videos using AI avatars — no camera, studio or acting required. Type your script, choose an AI presenter from 150+ diverse avatars across 120+ languages, and Synthesia generates a realistic video of the avatar delivering your content with natural lip-sync.

Widely used by corporate L&D teams, HR departments and marketing teams, Synthesia is particularly valuable for organisations producing high volumes of training, onboarding and compliance videos where traditional video production would be prohibitively expensive or time-consuming to update.

Who is Synthesia for?

Corporate L&D and HR teams creating training and onboarding videos at scale, marketing teams producing multilingual product explainers, and any business that needs to produce regular video content without the cost and logistics of traditional video production.

Frequently Asked Questions
Synthesia does not have a permanent free plan. A free trial or demo is available — contact Synthesia's sales team or visit their website to access it. Paid plans start at $29/month for the Starter tier.
Synthesia's avatars produce natural lip-sync and are generally convincing for training and corporate content. For highly customer-facing or emotional content, human video remains more natural — AI avatars work best for informational content where the script matters more than personal connection.
Both offer similar AI avatar video capabilities. Synthesia has a longer track record and stronger enterprise focus. HeyGen is often considered stronger for marketing-focused video content and has competitive pricing. Both are worth evaluating with a trial before committing.
