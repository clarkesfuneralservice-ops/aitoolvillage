What is HubSpot CRM?

HubSpot CRM is the world's most widely adopted CRM platform — used by 200,000+ businesses. Unlike Salesforce which targets enterprises, HubSpot built its reputation on being accessible to small and mid-size businesses, with a genuinely useful free tier that includes unlimited contacts, deal tracking, email templates and a sales pipeline.

HubSpot AI spans the entire platform — it writes sales emails and follow-ups, scores leads based on engagement, summarises calls and meetings, forecasts deal close probability and suggests the next best action for each prospect. The AI Content Assistant generates marketing copy, landing pages and social posts directly within HubSpot.

What makes HubSpot uniquely powerful is that CRM, marketing, sales and customer service all share the same data — so marketing can see which leads converted to revenue, sales can see every marketing touchpoint a prospect had and service can see the full sales history. Most competitors require separate tools and integrations to achieve this.
