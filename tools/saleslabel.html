What is SalesLabel?

SalesLabel is a B2B sales intelligence platform that provides real-time buying signals on target accounts — helping sales teams know when prospects are actively in-market and ready for a conversation, rather than relying on static CRM data or arbitrary sequence-based cadences. It's built specifically for RevOps teams and sales leaders who want their reps spending time on the highest-probability opportunities at precisely the right moment.

The core problem SalesLabel solves is timing. Most B2B sales outreach is poorly timed — reps follow a sequence regardless of whether the prospect is currently evaluating solutions in that category. SalesLabel monitors target accounts for signals indicating active buying behaviour: job changes, technology stack shifts, funding events, hiring patterns and content consumption — and surfaces these to reps as prioritised, contextualised actions.

For RevOps teams, SalesLabel sits on top of existing CRM and sales engagement tools as an intelligence layer rather than a replacement. It enriches your current workflow with real-time account context so pipeline prioritisation is based on current buying signals rather than last quarter's firmographic fit alone. The result is sales capacity directed at accounts that are genuinely active right now.

Who is SalesLabel for?

SalesLabel is designed for B2B sales teams at companies selling to enterprise or mid-market accounts where the sales cycle is long enough that timing intelligence provides meaningful advantage. It delivers the most value when you have a defined set of target accounts — an account-based selling motion — and want real-time visibility into which accounts are most worth pursuing this week versus next quarter.

Frequently Asked Questions
Buying signals are observable behaviours and events indicating a company may be actively evaluating or preparing to purchase a solution. Examples include: researching competitors or relevant topics online, posting job listings suggesting a new initiative, receiving funding enabling new investments, key decision-maker job changes, technology stack changes and engagement with relevant content. SalesLabel monitors these signals on your target accounts so reps know when the timing is right to reach out rather than guessing.
ZoomInfo and Apollo primarily provide contact data and company firmographics — who to reach out to and their contact details. SalesLabel focuses on timing intelligence — when to reach out based on real-time buying signals. The tools are complementary: Apollo or ZoomInfo tells you who your target contacts are; SalesLabel tells you which accounts are showing active buying behaviour right now. Most revenue teams use both types together for best results.
SalesLabel is primarily designed for B2B sales teams with a structured outbound programme and defined target account lists — typically companies with at least a small dedicated sales team running account-based motions. For very small businesses doing occasional outreach, simpler and less expensive tools may provide better ROI. SalesLabel's value scales with your target account list size and the volume of outbound your team executes.
