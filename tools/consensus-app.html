What is Consensus AI?

Consensus is an AI-powered academic search engine that answers your research questions using evidence from peer-reviewed scientific papers — not web pages, not blogs, not opinions. Ask any question and Consensus searches over 200 million scientific papers, extracts the relevant findings and shows you what the research actually says, with every claim linked directly to its source paper.

The standout feature is the Consensus Meter — a visual indicator that shows what percentage of relevant studies support, contradict or are inconclusive about a given claim. This makes it immediately clear whether scientific evidence is strong, mixed or limited on any topic, which is invaluable for researchers, students, healthcare professionals and anyone who needs evidence-based answers quickly.

Unlike Google Scholar which returns a list of papers to read, Consensus extracts the key findings for you. Instead of spending hours reading abstracts, you get synthesised answers in seconds — with the source papers linked so you can verify every claim. It's best described as Google Scholar meets ChatGPT, built for serious research.
