What is Rank Math?

Rank Math is the fastest-growing WordPress SEO plugin — used by 2 million+ websites and widely considered the best alternative to Yoast SEO. It provides comprehensive on-page SEO optimisation, schema markup, XML sitemaps, rank tracking and AI-powered content suggestions all within the WordPress dashboard.

Its AI-powered Content AI feature analyses your target keyword and suggests specific improvements to your content — recommended word count, related keywords to include, questions to answer and readability improvements. This is particularly valuable for bloggers and content teams who want data-driven guidance on what to write rather than guessing what Google wants to see.

Rank Math's free version is extraordinarily capable — it includes features that competitors charge for, like advanced schema markup (review schema, FAQ schema, recipe schema), Google Search Console integration, 404 monitor and redirect manager. The Pro version adds rank tracking, AI credits, local SEO and WooCommerce SEO for a fraction of competitors' prices.
