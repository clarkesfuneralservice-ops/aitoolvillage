What is Poe by Quora?

Poe is an AI platform built by Quora that gives you access to 100+ AI models — including Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4 (OpenAI), Gemini (Google), Llama and many others — from a single interface. Instead of paying for multiple AI subscriptions, Poe lets you access and compare all the leading models in one place for a single monthly fee.

Poe's key advantage is model variety — you can run the same prompt through Claude, GPT-4 and Gemini side by side to compare their responses. This is invaluable for users who want to find the best AI for each specific task, or who use different models for different types of work. You can switch models mid-conversation without losing context.

Poe also lets users create and share custom AI bots — configured with specific system prompts and model choices — which can be shared publicly or kept private. This makes it popular with power users who want customised AI assistants for specific workflows without building their own API integrations.

Frequently Asked Questions
Poe provides access to Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4 and GPT-4o (OpenAI), Gemini (Google), Llama (Meta), Mistral and 100+ other models including many community-created bots. The exact models available depend on your plan — the free plan includes several capable models with daily limits.
Poe has a free plan that gives daily message limits across a selection of AI models. Poe Pro at $20/month gives significantly higher message limits and access to all premium models including the latest Claude and GPT-4 versions. The free plan is enough to evaluate all the models available before committing to a subscription.
For users who want access to multiple AI models, Poe is better value — $20/month for access to Claude, GPT-4, Gemini and many others versus $20/month for ChatGPT Plus alone. The trade-off is that direct access to ChatGPT or Claude gives you the very latest features and model versions first. Power users who experiment with different models get more value from Poe.
