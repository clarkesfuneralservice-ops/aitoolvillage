What is Coursera?

Coursera offers 7,000+ courses from 300+ universities and companies including Google, IBM, Stanford and Yale. Its AI recommends personalised learning paths, adapts content to your pace and connects course completion to real career outcomes — making it the leading platform for professional upskilling.
