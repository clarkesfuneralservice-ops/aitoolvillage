What is Offrs?

Offrs is a predictive analytics platform for real estate agents — its AI analyses 250+ data points about every homeowner in a territory to predict who is most likely to sell their home in the next 12 months. Instead of cold-calling entire neighbourhoods, agents focus their prospecting on the 20% of homeowners the AI identifies as most likely to list.

The data points Offrs analyses include equity position, length of ownership, life events (marriage, divorce, new baby, death in family), job changes, local market conditions and historical selling patterns. By combining these signals, the AI identifies sellers with roughly 70% accuracy — meaning agents spend their prospecting time far more efficiently.

Offrs also includes SMART Leads — a done-for-you service where Offrs contacts predicted sellers on behalf of the agent via direct mail, digital ads and ISA calling, delivering hand-raisers who are actively considering listing.

Frequently Asked Questions
Offrs claims approximately 70% of the sellers in any given territory who do list will be identified by the AI in advance. This means agents focusing on Offrs-predicted sellers will encounter far more eventual listings per prospect than random cold-calling. No predictive system is 100% accurate, but 70% is a significant improvement over untargeted prospecting.
Offrs analyses over 250 data points including: property equity and ownership duration, life events (marriage, divorce, job change, new baby), mortgage data, neighbourhood sales activity, local price trends and historical selling patterns for similar properties. The combination of financial, demographic and market data is what makes the predictions more accurate than any single factor alone.
Offrs is primarily designed for listing-focused agents who want to identify motivated sellers before they list with another agent. Buyer's agents get less direct value from the platform. It's best suited to agents doing 10+ transactions per year who have a systematic prospecting process — the ROI improves significantly with volume.
