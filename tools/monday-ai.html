What is Monday.com?

Monday.com is a visual work management platform used by 225,000+ organisations including Coca-Cola, Universal Music and Hulu. Unlike traditional project management tools built around task lists, Monday.com uses a flexible board system where teams can manage projects, processes, clients and workflows in a highly visual, customisable way — no coding or technical expertise required.

Monday AI enhances the platform with AI-powered text summarisation (summarises long updates and thread discussions into key points), formula generation (writes complex column formulas from plain English descriptions), task automation suggestions (recommends automations based on how you work) and AI-generated sub-items that break large tasks into actionable steps automatically.

Monday.com's flexibility is its strongest differentiator — the same platform can run a marketing campaign calendar, a software development sprint, a client CRM, a recruitment pipeline and an event planning project simultaneously, each with their own custom columns, views and automations. Over 200 templates cover common use cases so teams can get started immediately without building from scratch.

Frequently Asked Questions
Monday.com is better for teams that need maximum visual flexibility and want to manage very different types of work (projects, CRM, HR, operations) in one tool. Asana is better for teams focused primarily on project and task management with strong workflow automation and reporting. Monday.com's boards are more customisable; Asana's interface is cleaner and easier to standardise across a team.
Monday.com does not have a permanent free plan — it offers a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. After the trial, plans start at $9/user/month (minimum 3 users) for the Basic plan. The Standard plan at $12/user/month is the most popular and includes timeline view, calendar, automations and integrations.
Monday AI can summarise long text updates and comment threads into key points, generate complex column formulas from plain English descriptions (e.g. 'calculate the number of days between start date and due date'), suggest automation recipes based on your board structure and generate sub-items to break large tasks into smaller actionable steps. AI features are available on Pro and Enterprise plans.
