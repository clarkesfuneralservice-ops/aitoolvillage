What is Monday.com?

Monday.com is a highly visual work management platform that makes project tracking accessible to non-technical teams. Its colourful, spreadsheet-like boards are easy to customise without any training — which is why it's used by 225,000+ organisations across marketing, operations, HR and sales.

Monday AI is built directly into the platform — it can generate task descriptions, summarise updates, auto-fill data columns, suggest automation recipes and even predict project completion dates based on historical team velocity. The AI assistant works across all board types without any setup required.

Monday.com's strength is its flexibility — the same platform can run a marketing calendar, a CRM, an IT ticket system or a product roadmap using the same building blocks. Its 200+ templates mean most teams can get up and running in under an hour.

Frequently Asked Questions
Monday.com is best for non-technical teams that need a flexible, visual way to manage projects and workflows. It's particularly popular with marketing, HR, operations and sales teams that want to manage work without learning a complex tool.
Monday AI is built into the platform and can generate task descriptions from a brief prompt, summarise board updates, auto-fill data columns using AI and suggest automation workflows. It also has an AI assistant that answers questions about your boards and helps you build workflows faster.
Monday.com is more visual and flexible — better for teams that want to customise their setup or use it across multiple departments. Asana has a cleaner, more opinionated structure — better for teams focused purely on task and project management. Both are excellent; the best choice depends on your team's working style.
Monday.com has a free plan for up to 2 users. For most teams, the Standard plan at $12/user/month offers the best value — it includes timeline view, automations and integrations.
