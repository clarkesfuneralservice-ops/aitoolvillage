What is Clay?

Clay is an AI-powered data enrichment and go-to-market automation platform that has rapidly become the tool of choice for modern sales and growth teams. It solves the fundamental problem of outbound sales: getting accurate, rich data about prospects and then writing personalised messages at scale without hiring a large SDR team.

Clay's waterfall enrichment feature searches up to 75+ data providers simultaneously — LinkedIn, Clearbit, Hunter, Apollo, ZoomInfo and many others — and pulls together the most complete contact and company profile possible from all available sources. Instead of paying for one expensive data provider and accepting its gaps, Clay queries multiple providers and fills in missing data from wherever it exists.

The AI writing layer then takes these enriched profiles and generates personalised outreach for each contact — referencing their recent LinkedIn posts, company news, job changes or any other data point Clay has collected. The result is outbound messages that read as individually researched and crafted rather than templated, dramatically improving reply rates. Clay integrates with all major email sequencing tools including Outreach, Salesloft and Instantly.

Who uses Clay?

Clay is used primarily by B2B sales teams, growth marketers and revenue operations professionals at tech companies. It's particularly popular at Series A-C startups that want enterprise-quality outbound without the headcount, and at agencies running outbound campaigns for multiple clients.

Frequently Asked Questions
Clay is primarily used for three things: enriching prospect lists with data from multiple sources, building hyper-personalised outreach at scale using AI, and automating go-to-market workflows like lead routing, CRM enrichment and trigger-based outreach. Sales teams use it to research prospects faster and write better cold emails; growth teams use it to automate data operations that previously required manual research or expensive data vendors.
Clay uses AI (typically GPT-4) to generate personalised outreach messages based on the enriched data it collects about each prospect. You provide a message template with instructions like 'write a personalised first line referencing their recent LinkedIn activity and company news' and Clay generates a unique first line for every contact using the data it has collected. The output reads as individually researched rather than templated, improving reply rates significantly.
For B2B sales teams doing outbound at scale, Clay typically pays for itself quickly. If your team spends 2-3 hours per day on prospect research and personalisation, Clay can reduce that to 30 minutes while improving the quality of outreach. At $149/month, one additional meeting booked per month from better-personalised outreach typically justifies the cost. For teams doing fewer than 500 prospects per month, cheaper alternatives like Apollo or LinkedIn Sales Navigator may be sufficient.
