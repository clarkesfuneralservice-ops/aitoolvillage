What is Reword?

Reword is an AI writing tool focused on helping writers improve existing content for reader engagement and retention — analysing articles for readability, structure and engagement patterns, then suggesting targeted improvements rather than generating content from scratch. It positions itself as a writing coach that helps human writers do better work, rather than replacing the writing process entirely.

The platform learns from articles that perform well for a given publication over time, using that context to make increasingly relevant suggestions tailored to a specific publication's style and audience.
