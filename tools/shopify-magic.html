What is Shopify Magic?

Shopify Magic is the suite of AI features built directly into the Shopify platform — available to all Shopify merchants at no additional cost. It generates product descriptions from bullet points, drafts email campaigns, creates FAQ answers for product pages and assists with storefront copy.

The most used feature is product description generation — upload a product, add a few bullet points and Shopify Magic writes a polished, SEO-friendly description instantly. For stores with large catalogues, this alone saves enormous amounts of time.

Shopify Magic also includes AI image editing — background removal, background generation and image enhancement for product photos. Combined with Shopify's built-in analytics, it gives store owners AI assistance across the most time-consuming parts of running an online store.
