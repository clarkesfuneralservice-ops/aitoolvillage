What is Tabnine?

Tabnine is one of the original AI code completion tools — launched in 2018, well before GitHub Copilot. Its key differentiator is privacy: Tabnine can run entirely on your local machine or private cloud, meaning your code never leaves your infrastructure. This makes it the top choice for enterprises in regulated industries like finance, healthcare and defence.

Tabnine's AI is trained exclusively on permissively licensed open-source code, which means it carries significantly lower IP risk than tools trained on all public code. For legal and compliance teams, this is a major advantage.

Beyond privacy, Tabnine offers strong whole-line and full-function completions, a chat assistant, test generation and code explanation features. Its team learning feature trains a private model on your organisation's codebase — so completions reflect your team's specific patterns and conventions over time.
