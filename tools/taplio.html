What is Taplio?

Taplio is an AI-powered LinkedIn growth platform that combines content creation, scheduling, analytics and relationship management in one tool. It's built specifically for LinkedIn — unlike general social media tools like Buffer or Hootsuite, every feature is designed around LinkedIn's content formats, algorithm behaviour and networking dynamics.

The AI content generator draws from a database of viral LinkedIn posts to suggest topics and generate content in your tone — covering text posts, carousels, polls and long-form articles. The scheduling tool lets you plan and queue weeks of content in advance, while the analytics dashboard shows which post formats and topics drive the most engagement and follower growth for your specific audience.

Taplio also includes a LinkedIn CRM feature for tracking relationships with key contacts — particularly useful for B2B sales professionals using LinkedIn for prospecting and deal pipeline management alongside personal branding.

Who is Taplio for?

LinkedIn creators growing a personal brand, B2B marketers producing thought leadership content, sales professionals managing LinkedIn outreach, and agencies managing LinkedIn accounts for multiple clients.
