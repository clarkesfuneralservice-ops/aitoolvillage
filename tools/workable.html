What is Workable?

Workable is an all-in-one recruiting platform used by 27,000+ companies including Spotify, Intercom and Airbnb to manage the entire hiring process — from writing job descriptions and posting to job boards, through candidate tracking and interview coordination, to offer management and onboarding. It's positioned as the most accessible enterprise-quality ATS for growing businesses that can't afford Greenhouse or Lever but need more than a spreadsheet.

Workable AI powers several key features that reduce manual recruiting work. The AI job description writer generates complete, inclusive job postings from a job title and a few key requirements — saving HR teams 30-45 minutes per role. AI candidate sourcing searches a database of 400 million+ passive candidates and surfaces people who match the role criteria, enabling proactive outreach rather than reactive application review. AI-powered interview question generation creates structured interview guides tailored to each role.

Workable's one-click job posting distributes roles to 200+ free and premium job boards simultaneously — including LinkedIn, Indeed, Google for Jobs, Glassdoor and industry-specific sites. This multi-board posting from a single platform eliminates the need to manually post to each board individually, saving significant time for every new role. The candidate pipeline view gives hiring managers real-time visibility into where candidates stand across all open positions.

Workable vs Greenhouse vs Lever

Workable is easier to set up and more affordable than both Greenhouse and Lever — it's the natural first professional ATS for companies that have outgrown spreadsheet recruiting. Greenhouse is better for companies needing structured hiring processes with rigorous interview kits. Lever is better for teams that prioritise talent pipeline management. Workable hits the sweet spot for growing businesses needing a capable, affordable ATS without enterprise complexity.

Frequently Asked Questions
Workable is designed for growing businesses — typically 50-1,000 employees with 5-50 open roles at any time. For very small businesses hiring 1-2 people per year, the $189/month cost is hard to justify and simpler tools like LinkedIn or Indeed's built-in ATS are more cost-effective. For companies hiring regularly (5+ roles per year), Workable's time savings on job posting, sourcing and scheduling typically justify the monthly cost quickly.
Workable's AI sourcing searches a database of 400 million+ professional profiles from across the web — LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio sites and other sources — and ranks candidates by match quality against your job requirements. You provide the role criteria and Workable surfaces passive candidates who match, allowing you to send personalised outreach directly from the platform. This is separate from inbound applications and helps fill roles where application volume is low.
Choose Workable if you want an easy-to-set-up, affordable ATS with good AI features for a growing business. Choose Greenhouse if you need rigorous structured hiring with detailed interview kits, scorecards and advanced analytics — typically suited to companies that have scaled to 200+ employees and are prioritising hiring quality over hiring speed. Most companies use Workable first and graduate to Greenhouse as their hiring processes become more formalised.
