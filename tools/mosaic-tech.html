What is Mosaic?

Mosaic is a financial planning and analytics platform aimed at startups and growing companies, connecting to accounting, HR and billing systems to provide real-time financial dashboards, scenario planning and forecasting without requiring complex spreadsheet models. It's positioned as a strategic finance tool for founders, CFOs and finance teams who need visibility into metrics like burn rate, runway and revenue projections.

For startups managing investor reporting and internal financial planning, Mosaic automates the data aggregation that traditionally requires manual spreadsheet updates from multiple systems, providing real-time visibility into key SaaS and startup metrics.
