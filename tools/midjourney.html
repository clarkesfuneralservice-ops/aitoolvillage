What is Midjourney?

Midjourney is an AI image generation tool known for producing the highest quality, most aesthetically refined images of any AI art generator. It operates via a web interface (previously Discord-only) and generates 4 image variations from a text prompt in about 60 seconds.

For marketers, Midjourney is used for creating ad visuals, social media imagery, blog post headers, product concept art and brand photography that would otherwise require expensive photo shoots or stock imagery. Quality is consistently better than Canva AI, DALL-E and Stable Diffusion for photorealistic and artistic outputs.

    • Frequently Asked Questions

    Does Midjourney have a free trial?
    No — Midjourney removed its free trial. The cheapest plan is $10/mo for 200 images. If you want free AI images, try Leonardo AI (150 credits/day free) or Bing Image Creator (free, unlimited).
    What is the best Midjourney plan for marketers?
    The Standard plan at $30/mo is the best value — it gives 900+ images per month in relax mode and 15 hours of fast GPU time. Most marketers don't need more than this.
    How do I get the best results from Midjourney?
    Use detailed prompts with style references, lighting descriptions and aspect ratios. Include terms like 'photorealistic', 'cinematic lighting', '4K' and reference specific artistic styles. The Midjourney community Discord has millions of prompt examples to learn from.
