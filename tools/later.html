What is Later?

Later is a social media scheduling platform with a strong visual focus — its drag-and-drop calendar and Instagram grid preview let you plan and visualise how your social feed will look before posts go live. Popular particularly among e-commerce brands, photographers and lifestyle creators for whom visual feed coherence matters, Later supports scheduling across Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, Twitter/X, Facebook and LinkedIn.

Later includes AI caption writing, hashtag suggestions, best-time-to-post recommendations and a link-in-bio tool, making it a well-rounded choice for creators and small brands who prioritise visual content planning and Instagram-first social strategy.
