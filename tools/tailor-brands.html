What is Tailor Brands?

Tailor Brands is an AI-powered branding platform used by 40 million+ businesses worldwide to create professional logos and brand identities without design skills or a designer. You answer a few questions about your business — name, industry, style preferences — and the AI generates multiple logo options in minutes. You select and customise your preferred design, then Tailor Brands builds a complete brand kit around it.

The brand kit includes social media post templates formatted for Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn and Twitter, business card designs, letterhead, email signatures and a brand style guide — all automatically generated in your brand's colours and typography. This makes Tailor Brands significantly more comprehensive than simple logo generators like Canva's logo maker, which only produces the logo itself.

Tailor Brands also offers business formation services — LLC registration, registered agent services and EIN application — making it a one-stop shop for entrepreneurs starting a new business. This combination of branding and legal business setup is unique in the market and particularly appealing to first-time business owners who need everything in one place.

Tailor Brands vs Canva for logos

Canva gives you more design control but requires manual work — you choose a template and edit it yourself. Tailor Brands generates a logo automatically from your brand description. Tailor Brands is faster and includes a more comprehensive brand kit; Canva gives more creative control for those with design sensibility. For complete beginners, Tailor Brands produces better results faster.

Tailor Brands lets you preview your generated logo for free — but downloading high-resolution files and accessing the brand kit requires a paid plan. Plans start at $3.99/month (billed annually) for the Basic plan which includes logo files in PNG format. The Standard plan at $9.99/month adds vector files (SVG, EPS), social media kit and brand style guide. The Premium plan at $19.99/month adds business card designs, mock-ups and priority support.
