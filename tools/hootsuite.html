What is Hootsuite?

Hootsuite is the world's most widely used social media management platform — used by 800+ of the Fortune 1000 and 18 million+ users. It covers every major social network including Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube and Pinterest, letting teams schedule, publish, monitor and analyse all content from one unified dashboard.

Its AI features include OwlyWriter AI for generating social media captions and hashtag suggestions, best time to post recommendations based on your audience's activity data, and AI-powered social listening that monitors brand mentions, competitor activity and industry trends across the web and social media in real time.

Hootsuite's strength over simpler tools like Buffer is its enterprise capability — team workflows with approval processes (drafts reviewed before publishing), role-based permissions, social inbox for managing all comments and DMs across platforms and detailed analytics that justify ROI reporting to stakeholders. For agencies managing multiple client accounts, Hootsuite's organisation features are essential.

Frequently Asked Questions
Buffer is better for small businesses and solo creators — it's simpler, more affordable (free plan, $6/channel/month paid) and has a cleaner interface. Hootsuite is better for teams and agencies that need social listening, approval workflows, team permissions and enterprise analytics. If you're a one-person business, Buffer wins on value. If you're managing multiple client accounts or brands with a team, Hootsuite's features justify the higher cost.
For agencies and enterprise teams, yes — Hootsuite's social listening, team collaboration and comprehensive analytics provide genuine value at scale. For small businesses spending less than $500/month on social media management, the $99+/month cost is hard to justify when Buffer or Sprout Social alternatives offer most key features at lower price points.
Hootsuite supports Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest and Google My Business. It also monitors the web for brand mentions beyond social media. The exact networks available depend on your plan tier.
