What is Speak AI?

Speak AI is an AI-powered transcription and analysis platform that converts audio, video and text into searchable, queryable data. Upload a recording, paste a URL or connect your calendar — Speak transcribes the content and then lets you extract insights, identify themes and generate summaries using AI Magic Prompts.

Beyond basic transcription, Speak's real differentiator is what happens after the transcript is generated. Its Explore Insights feature visualises patterns across large volumes of data, and Magic Prompts lets you ask natural language questions across your transcribed content — "what were the most common objections?" or "summarise the themes across these 50 interviews" — making it powerful for researchers, marketers, UX teams and businesses analysing customer conversations at scale.

Speak AI also includes a Meeting Assistant for Zoom, Microsoft Teams and Google Meet that automatically joins calls to transcribe and analyse them, plus Zapier integration for connecting transcription workflows to other tools.

Who is Speak AI for?

Speak AI suits researchers analysing interview data, marketers extracting insights from customer calls, UX teams processing user research, podcasters needing transcripts, and any business that regularly captures audio or video and wants to extract structured insights at scale.
