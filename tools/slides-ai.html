What is Slides AI?

Slides AI is a Google Slides add-on that uses AI to generate complete presentations directly inside Google Slides — the world's most widely used presentation tool. Instead of starting from a blank slide or spending hours in PowerPoint, you describe what you want, paste in a document or provide a URL, and Slides AI generates a full slide deck with structure, content, speaker notes and design in minutes.

The tool integrates directly into your existing Google Workspace workflow. There's no separate app to learn — you install it as a Google Slides add-on and it appears as a sidebar within Slides. This makes it the most accessible AI presentation tool for the 3 billion+ people who already use Google Workspace, removing the friction of adopting an entirely new platform.

Slides AI is particularly useful for consultants, marketers and educators who regularly need to create presentations from documents, research or briefs. You can paste in a 2,000-word document and receive a structured 15-slide presentation with the key points extracted and laid out coherently. The AI also generates speaker notes for each slide, giving presenters a ready-made script to work from.

Who uses Slides AI?

Slides AI is popular with business consultants who need to rapidly produce client presentations from research and reports, marketing teams creating campaign presentations from briefs, teachers and professors building lecture slide decks from course material, and students producing academic presentations from notes and papers.

Frequently Asked Questions
Slides AI is installed as a Google Workspace add-on. Once installed, you access it from the Extensions menu in Google Slides. You input your content — text, a document, a URL or a topic description — choose a design theme and number of slides, and the AI generates the complete presentation within your Google Slides file. You can then edit any slide as you normally would in Google Slides.
Slides AI has a free plan that includes 3 AI-generated presentations per month with basic features. Paid plans start at $10/month for 10 presentations per month and additional features including more design themes, longer presentations and priority generation. The free plan is enough for occasional users to evaluate the quality before committing to a subscription.
Slides AI is best for Google Workspace users who want AI presentation generation without leaving Google Slides. Tome is better for visually rich, narrative-driven presentations with a more modern aesthetic. Beautiful.ai is better for teams needing consistent professional design with smart templates. The choice depends primarily on your workflow — if you live in Google Slides, Slides AI is the most practical option.
