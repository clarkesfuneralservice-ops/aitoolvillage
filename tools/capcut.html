What is CapCut?

CapCut is the world's most downloaded video editing app — created by ByteDance (TikTok's parent company) and used by 200 million+ creators worldwide. Unlike most free video editors, CapCut has no watermark on exports and no essential features locked behind a paywall, making it genuinely free for everyday use.

Its AI features include auto-captions that transcribe and style subtitles automatically in multiple languages, AI background removal that works on mobile in real time, Auto Reframe that converts horizontal video to vertical for TikTok/Reels, and an extensive library of AI-powered effects, transitions and trending templates that update weekly to match current social media trends.

CapCut's integration with TikTok is seamless — trending sounds, effects and templates from TikTok appear in CapCut and content can be exported directly to TikTok. For creators focused on short-form social content, CapCut provides 90% of what professional tools offer at zero cost. The desktop version adds more advanced features for creators who need them.

For most personal content creators this isn't a concern. For businesses handling sensitive content or operating in regions with restrictions on ByteDance products, consider alternatives like Kapwing or Descript.
Use CapCut if you're creating short-form social content (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) and want a free, mobile-friendly tool with trending templates.
