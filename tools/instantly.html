What is Instantly?

Instantly is a cold email outreach platform focused on deliverability and scale — enabling sales teams and agencies to send personalised cold email campaigns to large prospect lists while maintaining inbox placement rates. Its warmup infrastructure (sending small volumes through a network of accounts to build sender reputation) addresses the core challenge of cold email — getting into the inbox rather than spam.

AI features include email personalisation from prospect data, subject line generation and sequence optimisation based on campaign performance data. Instantly is particularly popular with B2B agencies and sales teams running high-volume outbound campaigns.
