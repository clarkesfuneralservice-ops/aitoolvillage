What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI chatbot built by OpenAI, launched in late 2022 and now used by over 200 million people worldwide. It uses large language models (LLMs) to understand and generate human-like text, making it useful for writing, research, coding, analysis and general problem-solving.

The free version uses GPT-4o mini which is still extremely capable for most tasks. ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) unlocks the full GPT-4o model, image generation via DALL-E and access to GPT-4o with browsing. For marketers it's a powerful writing and brainstorming tool. For accountants it can help draft client communications, analyse data and explain complex regulations.

    • Frequently Asked Questions

    Is ChatGPT free?
    Yes — ChatGPT has a permanently free plan using GPT-4o mini. You don't need a credit card. ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) unlocks the full GPT-4o model and DALL-E image generation.
    Is ChatGPT good for marketing?
    Yes. Marketers use ChatGPT to write blog posts, ad copy, email campaigns, social media captions, product descriptions and more. It works best when you give it detailed, specific prompts.
    What is the difference between GPT-4o and GPT-4o mini?
    GPT-4o is OpenAI's most capable model — better at complex reasoning, longer documents and nuanced writing. GPT-4o mini is faster and free but slightly less capable. For most everyday tasks, GPT-4o mini is more than sufficient.
