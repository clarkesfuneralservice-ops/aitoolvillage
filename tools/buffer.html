What is Buffer?

Buffer is one of the most popular social media management tools for small businesses and content creators — used by 140,000+ businesses to schedule posts across Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok and Pinterest from a single dashboard. Its free plan covering 3 social channels is the most generous in the category.

Buffer's AI assistant generates social media captions tailored to each platform's style and character limits from a brief description of what you want to post. You describe your content and it produces multiple caption options — you choose the best one and schedule it. This dramatically reduces the time spent writing individual captions for each platform.

Its analytics show which posts perform best by reach, engagement and clicks — helping you understand what content resonates with your audience and when to post for maximum visibility. Buffer's Start Page feature lets you create a simple link-in-bio landing page for Instagram, making it a complete social media toolkit for creators and small businesses.
