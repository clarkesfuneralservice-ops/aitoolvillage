What is Kajabi?

Kajabi is the leading all-in-one platform for knowledge entrepreneurs — coaches, course creators, consultants and experts who sell their expertise online. It replaces the typical stack of Teachable + Mailchimp + ClickFunnels + Calendly + Stripe with a single platform that handles all of it natively.

Its AI features include Kajabi AI Creator Hub — which generates course outlines, lesson scripts, email sequences, sales page copy and social media content from a brief description of your topic. For coaches launching new programmes, this dramatically reduces the time from idea to launch.

Kajabi's business model is built around creator success — it takes no transaction fees (unlike Teachable or Thinkific), gives you full ownership of your customer relationships and provides built-in analytics showing revenue, student progress and email performance in one dashboard.
