What is Vidyo.ai?

Vidyo.ai — now rebranded as Quso AI — is an AI-powered video repurposing platform that automatically identifies the most engaging moments from long-form video content and converts them into short clips optimised for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels and LinkedIn.

Upload a podcast episode, webinar, interview or YouTube video and Vidyo's AI analyses the content for engagement potential — generating clips complete with animated captions, auto-resized aspect ratios and social-ready formatting. What used to take hours of manual editing takes minutes.
