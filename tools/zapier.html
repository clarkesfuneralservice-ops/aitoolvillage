What is Zapier?

Zapier is a no-code automation platform that connects apps together using 'Zaps' — automated workflows triggered by events. For example: when someone fills in a Google Form, automatically send them an email and add them to your CRM. With 6,000+ app integrations it connects almost any software combination you use.

For marketers, Zapier automates lead capture, email sequences, social posting and CRM updates. For accountants it can automate invoice processing, expense alerts and client onboarding. The free plan gives 100 tasks per month and 5 Zaps — enough to automate your most repetitive tasks without paying anything.

    • Frequently Asked Questions

    Is Zapier free?
    Yes — Zapier has a free plan with 100 tasks per month and 5 Zaps. This is enough for small automations. Paid plans start at $20/mo for 750 tasks.
    What is a Zap?
    A Zap is an automated workflow in Zapier. It has a trigger (something that starts the automation) and one or more actions (things Zapier does automatically). For example: 'When a new lead fills my form, add them to Mailchimp.'
    Is Zapier or Make better?
    Make (formerly Integromat) is more powerful and better value for complex workflows. Zapier is easier to use and has more integrations. For beginners, start with Zapier. For advanced automation, consider Make.
