What is Mailchimp?

Mailchimp is the world's most widely used email marketing platform — used by 12 million+ businesses worldwide. It's the go-to choice for small businesses, bloggers, nonprofits and creators starting with email marketing because of its generous free plan (up to 500 contacts) and ease of use that requires no technical knowledge.

Its AI features include subject line optimisation that suggests high-performing subject lines based on your audience's behaviour, send time optimisation that learns when each subscriber is most likely to open and Content Optimiser that analyses your email copy and design against best practices. These AI features improve open rates and click rates without requiring any marketing expertise.

Mailchimp's automation templates cover the most common email sequences — welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase follow-up and re-engagement campaigns — with pre-built workflows that small businesses can activate in minutes. For businesses ready to grow beyond the free plan, paid tiers add advanced segmentation, A/B testing and multivariate campaigns.
