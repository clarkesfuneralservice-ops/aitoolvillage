What is AdCreative.ai?

AdCreative.ai is an AI platform that generates complete ad creatives — both visuals and copy — for Facebook, Instagram, Google and other ad platforms. It's trained on billions of ad impressions and uses that data to predict which creative combinations will perform best before you spend a single dollar testing.

What makes AdCreative.ai unique is its performance score — every generated creative gets a predicted conversion score based on historical ad performance data. You can generate 10 variations and immediately see which ones the AI predicts will outperform, letting you launch with confidence rather than guessing.

For ecommerce brands running paid social, AdCreative.ai dramatically reduces the time spent in design tools and eliminates the need for a designer for most ad formats. It connects to your product catalogue and brand assets to generate on-brand creatives at scale.
