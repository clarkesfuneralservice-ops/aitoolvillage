What is Kroma?

Kroma is an AI-powered brand identity platform that generates a complete visual brand from a simple text description of your business. You describe your company — its name, industry, values and target audience — and Kroma generates a full brand identity package including a logo, colour palette, typography system, social media templates and brand guidelines document, all in a consistent visual style.

For startups and small businesses that can't afford a branding agency, Kroma provides a professional starting point in minutes rather than weeks. The generated brand assets are immediately usable — the logo exports in SVG and PNG formats, the colour palette comes with hex codes, and the social media templates are formatted for Instagram, LinkedIn and Facebook.

Kroma's AI learns from your feedback — if you don't like the initial generation, you can refine it by describing what you want differently, and the AI adjusts the design direction. This iterative approach means you can arrive at a brand that genuinely feels right for your business without needing to articulate specific design preferences in technical terms.

What's included in a Kroma brand package?

A complete Kroma brand generation includes a primary logo and variations (horizontal, stacked, icon-only), a colour palette with 5-6 colours and hex codes, typography pairing recommendations, social media post templates, email signature design and a brand guidelines document that explains how to use all assets consistently.
