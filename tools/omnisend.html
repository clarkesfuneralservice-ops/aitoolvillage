What is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an email and SMS marketing platform built specifically for ecommerce — it's a strong Klaviyo alternative that offers most of the same core features at a lower price point. Used by 100,000+ ecommerce brands, Omnisend focuses on automation flows that recover revenue and drive repeat purchases: cart abandonment, browse abandonment, post-purchase sequences and win-back campaigns.

Its AI product recommender automatically inserts personalised product suggestions into emails based on each customer's browsing and purchase history — without manual segment building. This powers the kind of personalised marketing that was previously only available to enterprise brands with dedicated data teams.

Omnisend's unified messaging approach lets you send email, SMS and push notifications from the same automation workflow — so a cart abandonment sequence can start with an email, follow up with an SMS and finish with a push notification, all triggered by the same event and managed in one place.
