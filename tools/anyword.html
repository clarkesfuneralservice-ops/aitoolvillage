What is Anyword?

Anyword is an AI copywriting platform with a unique feature no other tool offers: a predictive performance score that estimates how well your copy will convert before you publish it. Every piece of copy — ad headline, email subject line, landing page — gets scored against Anyword's database of billions of ad impressions to predict its likely conversion rate.

This makes Anyword particularly valuable for paid advertising teams who would otherwise spend thousands testing copy variations to find the winner. Anyword's AI generates multiple variations and scores each one, letting you launch with the highest-predicted performer from day one.

Beyond its scoring engine, Anyword includes a full AI writer for ads, emails, landing pages, blog posts and social media. Its brand voice feature learns your company's tone and ensures all AI-generated content stays consistent. It integrates with Google Ads and Facebook Ads for direct copy testing within the platforms.
