What is DALL-E 3?

DALL-E 3 is OpenAI's flagship image generation model, integrated directly into ChatGPT. It is exceptionally accurate at following detailed text prompts and excels at generating images with text, diagrams and specific compositional requirements. The free tier via ChatGPT provides limited daily generations, making it accessible to anyone with a ChatGPT account. DALL-E 3 via the API is also available for developers building image generation into their own products.
