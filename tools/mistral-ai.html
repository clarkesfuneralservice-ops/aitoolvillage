What is Mistral AI?

Mistral AI is a French AI company founded in 2023 by former Google DeepMind and Meta researchers — and has quickly become Europe's most prominent AI lab. Its open-source models (Mistral 7B, Mixtral 8x7B) are among the most capable freely available language models, often matching or exceeding much larger models in benchmarks while being far more efficient to run.

Mistral's key differentiator is its commitment to open weights — most of its models can be downloaded and run locally, meaning your data never leaves your own infrastructure. For organisations with strict data privacy requirements (healthcare, legal, finance) or those subject to GDPR who are concerned about data processing by US companies, Mistral offers a compelling alternative to OpenAI, Anthropic and Google.

Le Chat is Mistral's consumer-facing AI assistant — a clean, capable chatbot interface for everyday use. The Mistral API gives developers access to all models with competitive pricing. Mistral's enterprise offering includes dedicated deployments on European infrastructure, making it the preferred choice for European enterprises with data sovereignty requirements.
