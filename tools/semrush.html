What is Semrush?

Semrush is the industry-leading all-in-one digital marketing platform used by 10 million+ marketing professionals worldwide, including teams at Tesla, Samsung and IBM. It combines keyword research, competitor analysis, backlink auditing, site technical audit, content optimisation, rank tracking and social media management into one comprehensive platform.

Its keyword research database covers 25 billion keywords across 142 countries — giving you unmatched data for finding the right keywords to target. The Keyword Magic Tool lets you discover thousands of related keywords, filter by intent, difficulty and volume, and organise them into campaigns. For competitive research, Semrush shows you exactly which keywords any competitor ranks for, their top pages and their backlink sources.

Semrush's AI writing assistant and SEO Content Template generate optimised content briefs and first drafts based on top-ranking pages for your target keywords. Combined with the On-Page SEO Checker that analyses your existing content and provides specific improvement recommendations, Semrush gives content teams everything they need to create content that ranks.
