What is Sprout Social?

Sprout Social is an enterprise-grade social media management platform used by marketing agencies and in-house teams to manage publishing, engagement, analytics and social listening across multiple social channels from a unified dashboard.

Unlike more affordable tools like Buffer or Hootsuite, Sprout Social is positioned firmly at the enterprise and agency end of the market — it offers deeper analytics, more sophisticated team collaboration tools, CRM-style contact management and social listening capabilities that justify its higher price point for teams managing multiple brand accounts or clients at scale.

AI features have been added across the platform — including AI-assisted caption writing, optimal send time recommendations, automated inbox routing and AI-powered sentiment analysis in social listening reports.

Who is Sprout Social for?

Marketing agencies managing multiple client accounts, enterprise in-house marketing teams with several social channels, and brands that need deep analytics and social listening alongside publishing. The $249/month starting price makes it overkill for small businesses or solo creators — Buffer or Hootsuite serve those users better at much lower cost.

Frequently Asked Questions
Sprout Social is worth it for marketing agencies billing clients for social media management, and for enterprise teams where the analytics, social listening and team collaboration features justify the cost. For small businesses or solo creators, Buffer or Hootsuite deliver 80% of the value at a fraction of the price — Sprout Social's premium is in the depth of its reporting and team workflow features that only matter at scale.
Sprout Social has stronger analytics, a more polished UI and better customer support — users consistently rate it higher for ease of use and reporting depth. Hootsuite is more affordable and has a broader app marketplace. For agencies prioritising client reporting and team collaboration, Sprout Social is the stronger choice. For budget-conscious teams needing solid scheduling across many channels, Hootsuite is competitive at a lower price.
Sprout Social does not have a permanent free plan but offers a 30-day free trial on all plans — no credit card required. This is enough time to evaluate the publishing, analytics and team features thoroughly before committing to the $249/month Standard plan.
Sprout Social supports Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, Pinterest, TikTok, YouTube and Google Business Profile. The number of social profiles you can connect depends on your plan — Standard allows 5, Professional allows 10 and Advanced allows unlimited profiles.
Yes — Sprout Social is one of the best tools for agencies specifically. Its approval workflows let clients review content before publishing, custom reporting makes client presentations easy, and team collaboration features scale well across account managers and content creators. The per-user pricing can get expensive for large teams though — agencies should evaluate total cost against Hootsuite's team pricing at scale.
