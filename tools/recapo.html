What is Recapo.ai?

Recapo.ai is an AI-powered video repurposing platform that helps content creators transform long-form videos into short, shareable clips optimised for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts and other short-form platforms. Upload a long video — a podcast, YouTube video, webinar or interview — and Recapo's AI identifies the most engaging moments, generates scripts, adds subtitles and produces ready-to-publish short clips automatically.

What sets Recapo apart from similar tools is its combination of features in one workflow. Rather than using separate tools for clipping, subtitling, voiceover and editing, Recapo handles the entire repurposing process — from identifying highlight moments to adding AI voiceover — in a single platform. The chat-based editing feature is particularly useful: instead of using a traditional timeline editor, you describe what you want to change in plain English and the AI makes the edit.

The batch export feature makes Recapo well-suited for creators and agencies managing high content volumes — you can process multiple videos simultaneously and export a full week's worth of short clips in one session rather than editing each one individually. This dramatically reduces the time required to maintain a consistent posting schedule across multiple platforms.

Who is Recapo.ai for?

Recapo.ai is designed for YouTubers and podcasters who want to repurpose long content into short-form clips without spending hours editing, social media managers maintaining multi-platform content schedules, marketing teams repurposing webinars and event recordings, and agencies producing short-form content at volume for multiple clients.

Frequently Asked Questions
Recapo.ai uses AI to analyse the transcript and visual content of your uploaded video, identifying moments with high engagement signals — strong statements, questions, emotional peaks, topic changes and quotable moments. It then segments these into short clips of appropriate length for each platform (typically 30-90 seconds for TikTok and Reels, up to 60 seconds for Shorts). You can review the AI's selections and manually adjust which moments to include or exclude.
Recapo.ai offers a freemium model with a free plan that lets you evaluate the tool's clip generation and editing quality before committing to a paid subscription. The free plan has usage limits on video length, number of clips and exports per month. Check the Recapo.ai website for current plan details and pricing as these may have been updated since this review.
OpusClip is the most established AI clip generator with strong viral moment detection. Descript is better for full video editing with a text-based approach. Recapo.ai differentiates with its combination of AI voiceover, chat-based editing and batch export in one workflow — making it particularly strong for creators who need all these features without switching between tools. For pure clip generation, OpusClip has more of a track record; for an all-in-one repurposing workflow, Recapo is worth evaluating.
Recapo.ai supports major video formats including MP4, MOV and AVI uploads, as well as direct YouTube URL imports — paste a YouTube link and Recapo processes the video without downloading it first. Check the Recapo.ai website for the current full list of supported formats and maximum file sizes as these may vary by plan.
