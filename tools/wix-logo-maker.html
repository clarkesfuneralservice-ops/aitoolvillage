What is Wix Logo Maker?

Wix Logo Maker is an AI-powered logo generator built by Wix — the world's most popular website builder with 200 million+ users. It creates professional logos through a simple AI-guided process: you answer questions about your brand personality and style preferences, and the AI generates dozens of logo options for you to choose from, customise and download.

The AI design process takes about 5 minutes. You provide your business name and tagline, select your industry, choose from adjective pairs that describe your brand (e.g. 'classic vs modern', 'playful vs serious') and select visual styles you like. The AI uses these inputs to generate logo options that match your brand personality, which you can then customise — adjusting fonts, colours, icons and layouts in a drag-and-drop editor.

Wix Logo Maker is particularly good value for Wix website users — premium Wix website plans include logo files at no extra cost, meaning you get a complete website and logo package. For non-Wix users, the one-time download fee ($20-$50 depending on file types needed) is competitive with other AI logo services.

What's included in the download?

The Basic download ($20 one-time) includes high-resolution PNG and SVG files. The Advanced download ($50 one-time) adds a full brand kit with social media assets, favicon, business card template and brand colour palette. Both include commercial usage rights so you own the logo completely.

Frequently Asked Questions
Creating and previewing your logo with Wix Logo Maker is free. Downloading high-resolution files costs $20 (Basic — PNG and SVG) or $50 (Advanced — full brand kit). If you have a premium Wix website plan, logo files are included at no extra charge. You can preview your logo in full detail before paying anything, so there's no risk in trying.
You own full commercial rights to any logo you download from Wix Logo Maker. You can use it for your business, register it as a trademark and use it on all marketing materials without any ongoing licensing fees. Wix doesn't retain any rights to logos you create and download.
Both produce similar quality logos. Wix Logo Maker is better if you're already using or planning to use Wix for your website — you get the logo free with a premium plan. Tailor Brands is better if you want a more comprehensive ongoing brand kit with social media templates and a monthly subscription model rather than one-time payment. For pure logo quality, the difference is minimal.
