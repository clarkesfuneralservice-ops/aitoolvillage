What is n8n?

n8n is an open-source workflow automation platform that lets developers and technical teams build complex automations connecting hundreds of apps and services — similar to Zapier but self-hostable and with significantly more flexibility for technical users. It includes native AI and LLM integration nodes, making it popular for building AI-powered workflows and agents.

For technical teams wanting workflow automation without per-task pricing at scale, n8n's self-hosted option provides unlimited automation runs on your own infrastructure. Its visual workflow builder supports complex branching logic, custom code nodes and AI integrations that go beyond what consumer automation tools like Zapier typically allow.
