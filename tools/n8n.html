What is n8n?

n8n is an open-source workflow automation platform that connects 400+ apps and services through a visual node-based interface — similar to Zapier or Make, but self-hostable, more powerful for complex workflows and significantly cheaper at high volumes. It can be run entirely on your own infrastructure for free, with a cloud-hosted option for teams who prefer managed hosting.

What sets n8n apart from Zapier is its depth. Where Zapier excels at simple trigger-action automations built by non-technical users, n8n is designed for technical teams who need conditional logic, loops, custom code execution, webhooks and now AI agent capabilities — all in a single visual workflow builder.

n8n's AI agent features let you build LLM-powered workflows that can browse the web, query databases, call APIs and take actions based on AI reasoning — connecting models like GPT-4 and Claude to real business systems through the same node interface used for standard automation.

Who is n8n for?

Developers and technical teams who need powerful, customisable automation without Zapier's per-task pricing model, ops teams building complex multi-step workflows with conditional logic and custom code, and AI engineers building LLM-powered agents that interact with real business systems.
