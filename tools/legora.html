What is Legora?

Legora is an AI legal assistant built specifically for law firms — handling contract review, legal research, document drafting and due diligence with AI trained on legal documents and case law. Unlike general AI tools, Legora understands legal language, jurisdiction-specific requirements and the specific tasks lawyers perform every day.

The platform is designed to be deployed quickly without extensive IT overhead, making it accessible to mid-size and smaller firms that want to adopt legal AI without the enterprise procurement process required by tools like Harvey AI. Legora integrates with common document management systems and maintains strict data security and privilege protections.

Legora's core value is time savings on the most time-consuming legal tasks — contract review that might take a junior associate 3 hours can be completed in minutes, with Legora flagging key clauses, unusual terms, missing provisions and potential risks automatically.
