What is Lovo AI?

Lovo AI is an AI voiceover and video creation platform used by 500,000+ creators and businesses. What sets it apart from pure text-to-speech tools is its integrated video editor — you can generate an AI voiceover and immediately combine it with visuals, music and captions in the same platform without exporting to a separate video tool.

Its voice library includes 500+ AI voices in 100+ languages with a range of styles, ages and accents. The voices are notably expressive compared to basic TTS tools — capable of conveying emotion, emphasis and natural speech patterns. Voice cloning lets you create a custom AI version of your own voice or a branded voice for consistent content output.

Lovo AI is particularly popular with e-learning creators, YouTubers and marketing teams who produce high volumes of narrated video content. The Genny video editor lets you combine voiceover, stock footage, AI images, captions and music in one workflow — significantly reducing production time compared to using separate tools for each step.
