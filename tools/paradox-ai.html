What is Paradox (Olivia)?

Paradox is the company behind Olivia — an AI recruiting assistant used by McDonald's, Unilever, Amazon and 500+ other enterprises for high-volume hiring. Olivia is a conversational AI that engages candidates via text or chat, screens them against job requirements, schedules interviews and answers HR questions — all without human involvement.

For high-volume roles like retail, healthcare and logistics where companies hire hundreds or thousands of people per month, Olivia transforms the process. Candidates apply and can be screened, qualified and scheduled for an interview within minutes — not days. Time-to-hire drops dramatically and recruiter capacity increases because Olivia handles the repetitive work automatically.

Olivia integrates with all major ATS platforms including Workday, Greenhouse, iCIMS and Taleo — fitting into your existing hiring stack rather than replacing it. It handles multilingual conversations in 30+ languages, making it particularly valuable for global enterprises with diverse candidate pools.

Frequently Asked Questions
Olivia is an AI recruiting assistant that automates the early stages of hiring. When a candidate applies, Olivia immediately engages them via text or chat, asks screening questions, scores their responses against job requirements and — if qualified — schedules an interview automatically. It also answers candidate questions about the role and process 24/7. The goal is to remove the repetitive, time-consuming work from recruiters so they can focus on interviewing and selecting.
Paradox is primarily used by enterprises with high-volume hiring needs — retail, fast food, healthcare, logistics, manufacturing and hospitality. McDonald's, Amazon, Unilever and CVS are among its well-known customers. Companies that hire hundreds or thousands of hourly or entry-level workers per month get the most value from Olivia's automation. It's less suited to companies filling a small number of complex, specialised roles.
