What is FreshBooks?

FreshBooks is a cloud-based accounting and invoicing platform used by over 30 million people worldwide. Originally launched in 2003 as a simple invoicing tool, it has grown into a full accounting suite covering invoicing, expense tracking, time tracking, project management and financial reporting.

Unlike QuickBooks — which was designed for accountants — FreshBooks was built for business owners who aren't accounting experts. Every feature is designed to be intuitive, fast and painless. Creating a professional invoice takes under 2 minutes, sending automated payment reminders happens automatically and late payment fees can be applied with a single toggle.

In recent years FreshBooks has added AI-powered features including automatic expense categorisation, bank reconciliation and smart reporting — keeping it competitive with newer accounting platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FreshBooks good for small businesses?
Yes — FreshBooks is one of the best accounting tools for small service businesses, freelancers and consultants. It's particularly strong for invoicing, time tracking and getting paid faster. For product-based businesses needing inventory management, QuickBooks is a better fit.
Does FreshBooks have a free plan?
FreshBooks does not have a permanently free plan, but it offers a 30-day free trial with no credit card required. You get full access to all features during the trial. After 30 days, plans start from $17/mo. FreshBooks also frequently runs promotions offering 60-80% off for the first 6 months.
How does FreshBooks compare to QuickBooks?
FreshBooks is easier to use and better for invoicing — it's designed for business owners, not accountants. QuickBooks is more powerful for complex accounting needs, inventory management and multi-entity businesses. If you're a freelancer or service business, FreshBooks wins. If you have complex accounting needs, go with QuickBooks.
Can FreshBooks handle payroll?
FreshBooks does not have built-in payroll but integrates directly with Gusto for payroll processing. The integration syncs payroll journal entries automatically into FreshBooks, giving you a complete accounting and payroll solution.
Is FreshBooks worth it?
For freelancers and small service businesses — yes, absolutely. The time saved on invoicing and chasing payments typically pays for the subscription within the first week. The Lite plan at $17/mo covers most freelancer needs. Use the 30-day free trial to test it with your real clients before committing.
Does FreshBooks work on mobile?
Yes — FreshBooks has one of the best accounting mobile apps available. You can create and send invoices, capture receipts, track time, record expenses and view reports from your phone. Available on iOS and Android.
