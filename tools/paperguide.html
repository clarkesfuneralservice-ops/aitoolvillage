What is PaperGuide?

PaperGuide is an AI-powered research assistant designed specifically for students, academics and researchers who work with academic papers. It lets you upload any PDF — a research paper, journal article, thesis or textbook chapter — and then have a natural conversation with it. Ask questions, request summaries, extract key findings, get citations generated automatically and understand complex academic content faster than reading it manually.

The core value proposition is speed and comprehension. A typical academic paper takes 45-90 minutes to read, understand and extract key information from. PaperGuide reduces this to 5-10 minutes — you upload the paper, ask for a summary of the main findings, ask specific questions about methodology or results, and get accurate answers with page references in seconds. For researchers who need to review dozens of papers for a literature review, this is transformative.

PaperGuide also handles citation generation — a perennial pain point for students and researchers. It formats citations in APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard and dozens of other citation styles automatically from the papers you upload, eliminating the manual work of citation management and reducing formatting errors.

Who is PaperGuide for?

PaperGuide is ideal for university students writing dissertations and literature reviews, academic researchers conducting systematic reviews, professionals keeping up with research in their field, and anyone who regularly reads academic papers and wants to extract key information more efficiently. It's particularly valuable for interdisciplinary research where you need to quickly understand papers outside your core expertise.

Frequently Asked Questions
PaperGuide uses AI to process and understand the content of academic PDFs you upload. Once uploaded, the AI indexes the paper's content so you can ask questions about it in natural language. The AI searches the paper's content to find relevant answers and returns them with specific page references so you can verify the information in the original document. The citation generator reads the paper's metadata and formats it according to your chosen citation style.
PaperGuide is more accurate than general AI tools for academic paper content because it answers strictly from the uploaded document rather than from training data. Answers include page references so you can verify them in the original paper. Accuracy is highest for clearly formatted text-based PDFs — scanned papers or those with complex formatting may have lower accuracy. Always verify AI-generated summaries against the original paper for critical research work.
PaperGuide has a free plan that lets you upload and chat with PDFs with daily limits on questions and page counts. This is enough to evaluate whether the tool suits your workflow before committing to a paid plan. The Student plan at $9/month removes these limits and adds the research library and cross-paper search features most useful for sustained academic work.
PaperGuide is significantly better for academic paper analysis than ChatGPT. While ChatGPT can analyse uploaded PDFs, PaperGuide is purpose-built for academic content — it provides page-referenced answers (ChatGPT doesn't), generates accurate citations in academic styles, allows cross-paper comparison and is specifically optimised for the structure of academic papers. For serious research work, PaperGuide is the more capable and reliable choice.
PaperGuide processes uploaded papers to provide its AI features. Review PaperGuide's current privacy policy at paperguide.ai for detailed information on data handling, retention and whether uploaded content is used for model training. For highly confidential or unpublished research, review the privacy terms carefully before uploading sensitive documents.
