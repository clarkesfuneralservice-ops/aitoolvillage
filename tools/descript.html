What is Descript?

Descript is an AI-powered video and podcast editing platform built around a revolutionary concept — instead of editing a timeline with clips and waveforms, you edit the automatically generated transcript. Delete a word from the transcript and the corresponding audio and video are removed. Cut a paragraph and that section disappears from the recording.

Used by 500,000+ podcasters, YouTubers, course creators and video marketers, Descript has become the go-to tool for creators who want to produce professional video and audio content without learning complex editing software. Its AI features go far beyond transcription — Overdub clones your voice so you can correct mistakes by typing without re-recording, Studio Sound removes background noise automatically, and Filler Word Removal identifies and removes "um", "uh" and "like" with a single click.

Recent updates have added AI clip creation that identifies the most shareable moments for social media, eye contact correction that makes you appear to look at the camera even when reading a script, and a full screen recording tool for tutorials and demos.

Who is Descript best for?

Descript is ideal for podcasters who want to edit episodes quickly without timeline editing, YouTubers creating talking-head content, course creators producing educational video, marketers creating video ads, and anyone who records presentations or interviews and needs to clean them up professionally without hiring an editor.

Frequently Asked Questions
When you import a video or audio file into Descript, it automatically transcribes the content and displays the transcript alongside your media. Every word is linked to its exact timestamp. When you select and delete text in the transcript, Descript removes that exact portion of audio and video. You can also rearrange text to restructure your recording — making editing as intuitive as editing a document.
Descript has a free plan that includes 1 hour of AI transcription per month, text-based editing, filler word removal and basic export. Core AI features like Overdub voice cloning, Studio Sound and eye contact correction require the Hobbyist plan at $24/month. The free plan is enough to evaluate Descript's editing approach before committing.
Descript's AI transcription is very accurate for clear English speech — typically 95%+ accuracy for good-quality recordings with minimal background noise. Accuracy decreases with strong accents, multiple overlapping speakers, technical jargon and noisy environments. The transcription is fully editable so any errors can be corrected manually.
Use Descript if you're a content creator, podcaster or YouTuber who wants to edit quickly without learning complex software — its text-based approach makes editing fast and its AI features save significant time. Use Premiere Pro if you're a professional editor who needs precise multi-track editing, colour grading and complex effects. Many creators use Descript for rough cuts and cleanup, then export to Premiere for final polish.
Descript's Overdub voice cloning requires you to record a voice training script (about 10 minutes of speech) to create your voice model. Once trained, the AI generates your voice speaking any text you type — useful for fixing verbal mistakes without re-recording. Quality is generally very good for short corrections but varies depending on your voice characteristics and the quality of the training recording.
