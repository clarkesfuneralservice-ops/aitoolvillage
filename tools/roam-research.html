What is Roam Research?

Roam Research is a note-taking application built around bi-directional linking and networked thinking — notes link to each other both ways automatically, building an interconnected knowledge graph rather than a hierarchical folder structure. Popular among researchers, writers and knowledge workers who think in connections rather than categories, Roam encourages daily note-taking with automatic date linking and a graph view of note relationships.

While Roam is less AI-focused than some newer tools, its graph-based approach to knowledge has influenced a generation of knowledge management tools including Obsidian and Logseq, and it remains a dedicated tool for those who want outliner-based networked notes.
