What is Google Vertex AI?

Vertex AI is Google Cloud's unified platform for machine learning — providing tools to build, train, deploy and manage AI models including access to Google's Gemini models, AutoML for training custom models without deep ML expertise, and MLOps infrastructure for managing models in production.

For businesses building AI-powered applications on Google Cloud infrastructure, Vertex AI provides the foundational platform — from fine-tuning foundation models on proprietary data to deploying custom models at scale with monitoring and versioning. It's aimed at developers and data science teams rather than end-users seeking a consumer AI tool.
