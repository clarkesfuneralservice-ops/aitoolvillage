What is Bill.com?

Bill.com automates accounts payable and accounts receivable processes — businesses upload invoices (or have vendors email them directly) and Bill.com's AI extracts invoice data, routes it for approval, and processes payments. On the receivables side, it automates invoice creation, sending and payment collection from customers.

Widely used by accounting firms managing AP/AR for multiple clients, Bill.com integrates with QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite and other accounting platforms, syncing payment data automatically. Its approval workflows and audit trails are particularly valuable for businesses wanting better financial controls without manual paper-based approval processes.
