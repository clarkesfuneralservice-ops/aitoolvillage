What is Perplexity AI?

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search engine that searches the web in real time and synthesises answers with cited sources. Unlike ChatGPT (which has a knowledge cutoff), Perplexity always pulls fresh information. Unlike Google, it gives you a direct answer instead of a list of links to click through.

For marketers, Perplexity is invaluable for quick research, competitor analysis, finding statistics for blog posts and staying up to date with industry news. For accountants, it's useful for quickly understanding tax law changes, accounting standards and regulatory updates with source citations.

    • Frequently Asked Questions

    Is Perplexity AI free?
    Yes — Perplexity AI is free for unlimited basic searches. Pro Search (which does deeper, multi-step research) is limited on the free plan. Perplexity Pro ($20/mo) gives unlimited Pro Searches and access to GPT-4o and Claude models.
    How is Perplexity different from ChatGPT?
    Perplexity searches the web in real time and cites sources — it's always up to date. ChatGPT has a knowledge cutoff and can't browse the web on the free plan. Perplexity is better for research; ChatGPT is better for writing and complex reasoning.
    Can I trust Perplexity's sources?
    Generally yes — but always click through to the original sources for important decisions. Perplexity can occasionally mis-summarise or miss important nuance in source material.
