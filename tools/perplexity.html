What is Perplexity AI?

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search engine that searches the web in real time and synthesises answers with cited sources. Unlike ChatGPT (which has a knowledge cutoff), Perplexity always pulls fresh information. Unlike Google, it gives you a direct synthesised answer instead of a list of links to click through.

For marketers, Perplexity is invaluable for quick research, competitor analysis, finding statistics for blog posts and staying up to date with industry news — all with cited sources you can verify. For accountants and professionals, it's useful for quickly understanding regulatory changes and standards with authoritative citations.

Who is Perplexity for?

Researchers, content creators, marketers and professionals who need fast, cited answers to factual questions — particularly useful when currency and source credibility matter more than pure conversational generation.
