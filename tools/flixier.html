What is Flixier?

Flixier is a browser-based video editor that processes video in the cloud rather than on your local machine, making it significantly faster for rendering and exporting than traditional desktop software. It includes AI-powered features including automatic caption generation, AI voiceover, background removal and a library of templates for social media content — all accessible from any browser without downloading software.

Flixier is particularly useful for content teams and social media creators who need to collaborate on video projects, since multiple team members can work on the same project simultaneously from different locations. Its cloud rendering means even users on less powerful computers can edit and export high-quality video quickly.

Who is Flixier for?

Content creators, social media teams and businesses producing regular video content who want a fast, collaborative browser-based alternative to desktop editors like Adobe Premiere or DaVinci Resolve.
