What is Fireflies.ai?

Fireflies.ai is an AI meeting assistant that automatically joins your Zoom, Google Meet and Teams calls, records and transcribes in real time, generates smart summaries and syncs everything to your CRM. It's used by 500,000+ businesses and is particularly popular with sales teams for its deep CRM integrations.

What sets Fireflies apart from Otter.ai and Fathom is its CRM connectivity — it can automatically push meeting summaries, action items and call notes to Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive and other CRMs without manual copy-pasting. For sales reps who take 5-10 calls per day, this alone saves hours of admin every week.

Its AI search feature lets you find any moment from any past meeting by searching for keywords, topics or speaker names — turning your meeting history into a searchable knowledge base. The Conversation Intelligence layer analyses call sentiment, talk ratios and topic coverage for sales coaching.

Frequently Asked Questions
Fireflies is better for sales teams and anyone who needs CRM integration — it automatically pushes meeting notes to Salesforce, HubSpot and Pipedrive, which Otter doesn't do natively. Otter is better for transcribing audio files and podcasts, and has a cleaner interface for general transcription work. For business meetings with CRM follow-up, Fireflies wins.
Fireflies has a free plan that includes 800 transcription minutes per month, unlimited meeting storage (limited summaries) and basic integrations. The Pro plan at $10/month per seat adds unlimited AI summaries, CRM integrations and conversation intelligence. For most individual users, the free plan is sufficient to evaluate whether it fits their workflow.
Fireflies works with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Webex, GoToMeeting and most other major video platforms. It joins as a bot participant — you invite it to meetings or connect your calendar and it joins automatically. It also accepts audio file uploads for transcribing recorded calls and podcasts.
