What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is the world's most widely used CRM (customer relationship management) platform, used by businesses of all sizes to manage sales pipelines, customer data, marketing automation and customer service. Its Einstein AI layer adds predictive scoring, automated data entry, forecasting and natural language search across the CRM data.

For sales teams, Salesforce provides a unified view of every customer interaction, opportunity and activity, with AI that highlights which deals are most likely to close, suggests next best actions and automates routine data entry. The platform's vast ecosystem of integrations and AppExchange apps makes it highly customisable for different industries and processes.
