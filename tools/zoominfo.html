What is ZoomInfo?

ZoomInfo is the world's leading B2B go-to-market intelligence platform — used by 35,000+ companies including Google, Amazon and Zoom to identify, reach and convert their target buyers. It provides access to 300 million+ business profiles with verified contact data, company firmographics, technology stack information and buying intent signals that indicate which companies are actively researching solutions like yours.

ZoomInfo's intent data is its most powerful differentiator. By monitoring billions of web interactions across thousands of B2B content sites, ZoomInfo identifies companies showing research behaviour that signals they are in-market for specific categories of solutions. This allows sales and marketing teams to prioritise outreach to companies that are actively looking to buy rather than cold-calling the entire market blind.

The platform has expanded significantly beyond data — ZoomInfo now includes Engage (email and phone automation), Chorus (conversation intelligence that analyses sales calls), Chat (website chatbot) and MarketingOS (ABM automation). This expansion makes it a comprehensive revenue platform rather than just a data provider, competing directly with Salesforce, HubSpot and Outreach for enterprise sales team mindshare.

Is ZoomInfo worth the cost?

ZoomInfo is expensive — typically $15,000-$40,000+ per year depending on users and features. For enterprise sales teams with large outbound motions, the ROI is typically positive because better targeting reduces wasted prospecting time and intent data improves meeting conversion rates. For companies under $5M ARR or with small sales teams, Apollo, Lusha or Clay offer 80% of the value at a fraction of the cost.
