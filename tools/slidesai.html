What is Slides AI?

Slides AI is a Google Slides add-on that uses artificial intelligence to generate complete presentations automatically from text input — whether that's a typed description of your topic, a pasted document, a URL or uploaded PDF. It works entirely within Google Slides, appearing as a sidebar so there is no new platform to learn, no export/import friction and no disruption to your existing Google Workspace workflow.

The generation process is straightforward: you provide your content and choose a design theme and slide count, and Slides AI structures the information into a logical presentation format — title slide, section headers, content slides and a conclusion — with bullet points, supporting details and speaker notes for each slide. A full 15-slide presentation is generated in under two minutes, giving you a strong structural foundation to refine rather than a blank canvas to fill.

Slides AI is particularly valuable for professionals who regularly create presentations from research documents, meeting briefs or client reports. Instead of manually extracting key points and building slides, you paste the source document and receive a structured presentation ready for editing. Marketing teams use it to turn campaign briefs into presentations, consultants use it to turn research reports into client decks, and educators use it to turn course materials into lecture slides.

Who is Slides AI best for?

Slides AI delivers the most value to heavy Google Slides users who frequently need to produce new presentations from documents or briefs — consultants, marketing managers, teachers and business analysts. For users who primarily create presentations from scratch with custom design, tools like Beautiful.ai or Tome may offer more creative control.

Frequently Asked Questions
Slides AI is installed as a Google Workspace add-on from the Google Workspace Marketplace. Once installed, access it via Extensions → Slides AI in Google Slides. A sidebar opens where you input your content — text, document or URL — select your preferences and click Generate. The AI creates a complete presentation within your current Google Slides file within minutes, ready for you to edit and refine.
Slides AI has a free plan that includes 3 AI-generated presentations per month with access to basic design themes. Paid plans start at $10/month for 10 presentations and more themes. The free plan is enough for occasional users to test quality and workflow before committing. There is no credit card required to install the add-on and try the free tier.
Slides AI is best for Google Workspace users who want AI presentation generation without leaving Google Slides. Tome is better for visually rich, modern presentations with a narrative format. Gamma produces more polished designs with better visual variety. The key differentiator for Slides AI is the Google Slides integration — if you use Google Slides daily, Slides AI fits your workflow seamlessly.
