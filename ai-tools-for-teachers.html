Frequently Asked Questions

What AI tools can teachers use in the classroom?
ChatGPT for lesson planning and quiz generation, Canva AI for visual materials, Grammarly for writing feedback and Otter.ai for transcription are the most widely used AI tools among teachers. Most tools have free plans suitable for individual teachers.
Can AI create lesson plans?
Yes — ChatGPT creates detailed lesson plans in minutes when given the subject, grade level, topic and duration. Include learning objectives, differentiation strategies and assessment ideas in your prompt for better results. Teachers report saving 2–4 hours per week on planning.
Is AI allowed in schools?
Policies vary by institution and country. Most schools now have guidelines for AI use by both teachers and students. AI tools for teacher productivity (lesson planning, admin, research) are generally widely accepted. AI tools for student work are more restricted — check your school's specific policy.
