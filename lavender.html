What is Lavender?

Lavender is an AI email coach that works directly inside Gmail, Outlook and your sales engagement platform — scoring your cold emails in real time and suggesting specific improvements to increase reply rates. It's used by 50,000+ sales professionals at companies including LinkedIn, Zoom and HubSpot.

Unlike general AI writing tools, Lavender is purpose-built for cold email and sales outreach. Its AI analyses your email against millions of cold email data points and gives you a score out of 100, with specific feedback on subject line, email length, personalisation, readability, call to action and sentiment. You see exactly why your email might underperform before you hit send.

Lavender also includes a prospect intelligence panel that pulls data about your recipient from LinkedIn, news sources and company data — surfacing relevant personalisation opportunities without leaving your inbox. Sales teams using Lavender typically see a 2-3x increase in reply rates compared to emails written without AI coaching.
