How to Choose

On a budget ($0-50/mo)

Start with Rank Math (free WordPress plugin) + Perplexity AI (free research) + Frase ($15/mo for content briefs). This covers on-page optimisation, research and content planning at minimal cost.

For content teams ($100-200/mo)

Surfer SEO is the priority — its real-time content scoring directly improves rankings for new content. Pair with Writesonic or Jasper AI for drafting.

For full SEO operations ($300+/mo)

Semrush or Ahrefs for research and backlinks, plus Surfer SEO for content — the standard professional stack used by agencies and in-house SEO teams.
